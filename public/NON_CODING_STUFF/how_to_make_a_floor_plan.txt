1. list requirements
    what you need for the room

2. measure
    measure twice
    footprint of space
    doorways
    entries
    windows
    walls
        floor to ceiling
        wall to wall
        electrical outlets
        light switches
        any other switches
    permanent fixtures
        fireplaces
        mantles
        shelves
    any other objects inside the space

3. scale
    fit to screen
    common scale is 1/4 in = 1 foot

4. think about sightlines
    what will be visible from where?
    when sitting?
    when standing?
    windows, doors, walls are most important to consider
